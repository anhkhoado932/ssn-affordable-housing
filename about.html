Our Mission

At SSN Affordable Housing, our mission is to pioneer innovative solutions for sustainable and affordable housing. We believe that a sustainable housing future is possible only when the measurement of housing affordability considers sustainable housing supply.

Our Project: Housing Sustainability Indices (HSI)

Our project aims to construct Housing Sustainability Indices (HSI) for major Australian cities. This groundbreaking initiative is designed to revolutionize how we approach affordable and sustainable housing.

Key Features of HSI:

  • Reflects both social and physical/environmental challenges faced by the Australian housing market
  • Provides a measurement of affordability from environmental considerations and regulatory constraints
  • Helps local governments and developers identify potential areas to develop affordable housing
  • Assists in determining appropriate financing resources for these projects

Impact of HSI:

By developing these indices, we aim to provide a comprehensive tool that will:

  • Guide policy-makers in creating more effective housing strategies
  • Assist developers in identifying sustainable and affordable housing opportunities
  • Help communities understand and address their unique housing challenges
  • Promote a more holistic approach to housing affordability that considers long-term sustainability

Our Vision

We envision a future where sustainable and affordable housing is accessible to all Australians. Through our data-driven approach and the implementation of the Housing Sustainability Indices, we aim to transform the housing industry and create positive, lasting impacts on communities across the country.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We constantly seek new ways to improve housing affordability and sustainability.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of partnerships to drive meaningful change.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our endeavors.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to creating housing solutions that are environmentally responsible and energy-efficient.
